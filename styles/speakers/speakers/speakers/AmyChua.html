Amy Chua

Amy Chua is a professor at Yale Law School and a bestselling author. She is widely recognized for her book Battle Hymn of the Tiger Mother, which sparked national discussions on parenting, culture, and discipline.

Chua's work explores cultural identity, law, and international affairs, aiming to educate and challenge readers to think critically about social and familial norms.
