Andrew Ross Sorkin

Andrew Ross Sorkin is a financial journalist and author, best known for his work as a reporter for The New York Times and as co-anchor of CNBC's Squawk Box. He specializes in business, finance, and global markets, providing analysis of corporate strategies and economic trends.

Sorkin authored the bestselling book Too Big to Fail, exploring the 2008 financial crisis and the inner workings of Wall Street, making him a trusted voice in business journalism.
